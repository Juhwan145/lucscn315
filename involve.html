Our Ministry Teams

Korea School

As children grow up in the U.S., this school helps them to maintain their identity as Koreans, steadily learning their native language and writing skills. Through this, they develop the gift of bilingualism and grow as disciples of Jesus Christ, becoming precious workers for God’s kingdom.

VISCAT(Sunday School)

Children who take vision! From toddlers to elementary students, we help them grow in faith by learning God's Word so they can mature within God's vision.

SALTEEN(Youth)

“The future champions for Christ” ​ Our youth group is a worship ​ gathering to love God and to serve ​others.

GIL(College)

A healthy community growing together with young adults and college students through the love of God! In the vibrant season of youth, we experience God's love, share it with one another, and spread it to the world.

Small Grough(Adult)

A gathering of believers who live as Christ-like disciples, full of love and service, growing as mature members of the church! Through worship and small group fellowship, we share our faith and daily lives, working together to expand the Kingdom of God.
